About S.R. Robotics & Software Solutions

Founded in 2025, S.R. Robotics & Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd. strives to provide the highest quality education and cutting-edge solutions in robotics, AI, and software development. We aim to equip individuals with the skills and tools to thrive in a tech-driven world, offering diverse courses tailored to all skill levels.

Our team is dedicated to bridging the gap between education and industry by providing hands-on learning experiences, expert guidance, and continuous innovation in the tech space.

Our Mission

At S.R. Robotics & Software Solutions, our mission is to inspire and empower individuals through education in robotics, artificial intelligence, and software development. We strive to provide a platform where students, professionals, and businesses can unlock their potential and create meaningful, impactful technologies.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading global provider of educational resources in robotics and technology solutions. We aim to be at the forefront of innovations in AI and software, making a positive impact on industries worldwide while shaping the future of technology education.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Constantly pushing the boundaries of technology and education.
  • Excellence: Committed to delivering top-quality courses and solutions.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and ethics.
  • Collaboration: Fostering teamwork and partnerships to achieve shared success.
  • Customer Focus: Placing our customers at the heart of everything we do.
